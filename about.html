The Community Impact Club at Mount Carmel Area High School is a student-led organization committed to making a positive difference in our school and local community. Through projects like food drives, town clean-ups, and other student-led initiatives, we empower students to take meaningful action, develop leadership skills, and work together to solve real community problems. Our mission is to inspire service, create lasting impact, and foster a culture of collaboration, responsibility, and community pride among our members.
